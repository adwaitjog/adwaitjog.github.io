Course Description and Prerequisites

An introduction to the principles of computer design. Students will apply their knowledge of digital logic design to understand the high-level interactions between different computer system hardware components. The course will cover various computer architecture aspects related to CPU design, pipelining, memory hierarchy, and multiprocessor architecture. A tentative weekly agenda of topics can be found at the end of this web-page.

Prerequisite(s): CSCI 304 and either CSCI 301 or CSCI 303
