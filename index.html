About

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at The College of William and Mary (W&M) in Virginia, USA. At W&M, I am leading the Insight Computer Architecture Lab with the goal to generate insights, which would help in architecting future computers that are more energy-efficient, capable, reliable, and secure.

I am looking for self-motivated students to join our lab. Current topics of interest include (but not limited to): GPU Architectures, Approximate GPU Computing, GPU Reliability and Security, and Non-traditional Architectures (e.g., Automata Processor, Quantum Architectures).

If you are interested to join our lab, email me with your CV. If you are not currently at W&M, also apply here.
