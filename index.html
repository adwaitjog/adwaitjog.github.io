About

I am an Associate Professor (with Tenure) and Anita Jones Faculty Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. Before joining UVA in January 2023, I was the Adina Allen Associate Professor (with Tenure) in the Department of Computer Science at William & Mary.

I am leading the Insight Computer Architecture Lab and looking for motivated students interested in the general area of computer architecture & systems, including (but not limited to) accelerators, GPUs, quantum computing, reliability, or security.

A high-level summary of my group's prior work at William & Mary can be found here and here.
