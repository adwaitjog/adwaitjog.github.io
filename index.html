   Department of Computer Science and Engineering

   Penn State University, University Park

    adwait@cse.psu.edu, Google Scholar Profile


Starting Fall 2015, I will be an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the College of William and Mary (W&M) in Virginia. I will be starting a new computer architecture research group at W&M (US News Rankings), which will be working on fresh ideas with the goal of designing future computing systems more energy-efficient, reliable, secure, capable, and useful.

I am looking for highly self-motivated, talented, and creative students to work with, having interests in the broad areas of computer architecture, systems, software, and circuits. I am also excited to explore opportunities that can lead to solid cross-disciplinary research work. Therefore, I am also on the lookout for students having interests in other disciplines such as (but not limited to) biology, economics, machine learning, security, and theory.

Feel free to contact me if you are interested to know more or discuss the possibilities of joining my group.

I am currently a member of High Performance Computing Lab at Penn State. Before joining Penn State, I completed my undergraduate studies at NIT Rourkela, India.
