About

I am an Associate Professor (with Tenure) and Anita Jones Faculty Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. I am also an associate editor of IEEE Micro and IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing (TCC). My research interests lie in the broad area of computer architecture/systems with a special focus on GPUs and memory systems.
